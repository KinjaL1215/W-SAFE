🛡️ Safety Tips & Guidelines

×

🚶 While Walking

  • ✓ Stay aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts
  • ✓ Keep your phone charged and easily accessible
  • ✓ Walk with confidence and maintain good posture
  • ✓ Avoid using headphones in one ear — keep awareness
  • ✓ Share your location with trusted friends/family
  • ✓ Use well-lit, populated routes when possible
  • ✓ Walk in pairs or groups whenever you can

🚗 Vehicle Safety

  • ✓ Check around and under your car before approaching
  • ✓ Have keys ready before reaching your car
  • ✓ Lock doors immediately after entering
  • ✓ Park in well-lit, visible areas
  • ✓ Never leave your engine running unattended
  • ✓ Keep valuables out of sight in your car

📱 Digital Safety

  • ✓ Review privacy settings on social media regularly
  • ✓ Don't share your real-time location publicly online
  • ✓ Be cautious about meeting strangers from apps
  • ✓ Tell someone where you're going and when you'll be back
  • ✓ Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • ✓ Be aware of phishing and scam attempts

🏠 Home Safety

  • ✓ Always lock doors and windows, even when home
  • ✓ Use a door peephole or camera before opening
  • ✓ Don't let neighbors or strangers into your building
  • ✓ Change locks after a breakup or loss of keys
  • ✓ Install motion-sensor lights outside
  • ✓ Tell trusted people about unexpected visitors

🆘 In an Emergency

  • 🚨 Call emergency services (911 in US) immediately
  • ✓ Use W-SAFE's SOS feature to alert emergency contacts
  • ✓ Share your live location with trusted contacts
  • ✓ Try to remember details about threats (description, vehicle, etc.)
  • ✓ Move to a safe, public place if possible
  • ✓ Don't confront an attacker — prioritize your safety
  • ✓ Seek help from police, store staff, or other people nearby

💪 Self-Defense Tips

  • ✓ Take a self-defense class to build confidence and skills
  • ✓ The most important defense is awareness and avoidance
  • ✓ If attacked, make noise and draw attention
  • ✓ Target vulnerable areas (eyes, throat, instep) if necessary
  • ✓ Use your voice firmly — say "No!" with authority
  • ✓ Run toward people and lit areas if being followed

🛡️ Remember: Your safety and well-being are paramount. Trust your gut feeling, stay alert, and always prioritize getting to safety. W-SAFE is here to help you quickly connect with emergency contacts when you need them most.
